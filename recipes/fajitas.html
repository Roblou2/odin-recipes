Fajitas Recipe

Fajitas with a twist

This recipe for fajitas is dangerously tastey! You've been warned!

The BBQ sauce and cream cheese make it one to remember.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • Half an onion
  • Cream cheese
  • Smokey BBQ spice mix
  • A red pepper
  • Flour tortilla wraps
  • BBQ sauce
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Fry the onions in a bit of olive oil until brown.
  2. Cut the chicken up into small strips and fry with the onions.
  3. Cut up the red pepper and add to the pan
  4. Add half a pack of smokey BBQ mix or more/less depending on how hot you like your fajitas.
  5. Pop your tortilla wraps in the oven on a low heat for 5 minutes.
  6. Spread cream cheese onto each wrap.
  7. Add the fajita mix to the wrap.
  8. Squirt on some BBQ sauce.
  9. Wrap up your fajita and enjoy.
