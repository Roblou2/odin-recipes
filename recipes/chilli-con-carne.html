Chilli Con Carne Recipe

A true winter warmer for those chilli nights

Warm yourself up with this tastey chilli con carne recipe.

Ingredients

  • 500g beef mince

  • Half an onion

  • A few shreds of a grated garlic

  • 2 cans of chopped tomatoes

  • Can of kidney beans

  • Chilli powder

  • Salt

  • Basmati rice

  • Garlic powder

  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Fry the onions and garlic shreds in a bit of olive oil.

  2. Add a bit more olive oil if necessary and fry the beef mince until brown

  3. Drain the mix of any excess water or oil.

  4. Add the chopped tomatoes and kidney beans to your fried beef mince mix.

  5. Throw in 2 tsps of salt

  6. Add as much chilli powder according to your tastes.

  7. Put in a sprinkling of garlic powder

  8. Bring the mix to the boil.

  9. Simmer on a low heat for 2-3 hours.

  10. Serve with basmati rice.
